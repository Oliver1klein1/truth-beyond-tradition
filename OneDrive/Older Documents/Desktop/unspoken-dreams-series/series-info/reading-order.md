# The Unspoken Hopes and Dreams Series Reading Order

A poetic love letter from parent to child … written before the first breath, the first step, the first word.

1. **Whispers to My Unborn Son: A Mum's Poetic Life Jacket**
   - Image: `assets/covers/b1-mum-son2.jpg`
   - Before your son takes his first breath, you've already begun to shape his heart … with your love, your hope, your dreams for the man he'll become. This book captures that sacred whisper. Through simple, five-line rhymes and enchanting illustrations, a mother passes on courage, gentleness, and grace. A perfect baby shower or bedtime gift … a keepsake to treasure forever.

2. **Whispers to My Unborn Son: A Dad's Poetic Life Jacket**
   - Image: `assets/covers/b2-dad-son-cover.jpg`
   - Long before your son opens his eyes, your heartbeat is already teaching him strength and love. This book is your voice when words fall short … a father's legacy in verse. Each five-line poem becomes a compass for his soul, guiding him toward truth and tenderness. An unforgettable keepsake for new or expecting fathers … promising wisdom that lasts a lifetime.

3. **Whispers to My Unborn Daughter: A Dad's Poetic Life Jacket**
   - Image: `assets/covers/b3_dad_daughter_cover.jpg`
   - A daughter may not remember every word her father says … but she'll never forget how he made her feel: safe, seen, and loved. These verses are his embrace in rhyme … a father's quiet devotion captured in five lines at a time. Each poem plants seeds of confidence, compassion, and strength. A heartwarming gift for dads welcoming daughters … the beginning of a bond that lasts forever.

4. **Whispers to My Unborn Daughter: A Mum's Poetic Life Jacket**
   - Image: `assets/covers/b4-mum-daughter.jpg`
   - Before your daughter sees your face, she already carries your heartbeat within her. This book is your soul, written in lullabies. Through tender five-line poems and radiant illustrations, a mother weaves lessons of kindness, courage, and freedom. A gift every mum-to-be will cherish … a promise of love wrapped in poetry.

## Notes
- Perfect for parents to read with little ones aged 3–7
- Each book contains tender five-line verses
- These keepsakes bridge generations and preserve the most sacred moments of early parenthood
- Whether you're expecting or already holding your miracle, these books are a timeless gift from your heart to theirs
